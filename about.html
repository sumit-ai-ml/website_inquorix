Our Mission

We help companies hear customers at the speed of change. Inquorix pairs domain-trained AI with human context experts so every review, ticket, and transcript reveals what to fix, what to celebrate, and where to invest next.

Human-first insights

Anthropologists and linguists annotate real conversations, giving our models the empathy needed to respect each community we study.

Context-aware intelligence

Domain-tuned language models recognize jargon, sentiment shifts, and cultural references across regions, making qualitative signals measurable.

Actionable confidence

Narrative summaries and decision frameworks move teams from insight to roadmap without losing the emotional resonance of customer stories.
